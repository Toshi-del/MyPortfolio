Project Overview

The RSS Citi Healthcare Management System is a full-featured Laravel-based web application for managing all aspects of a healthcare clinic. It covers patient management, appointment scheduling, OPD and pre-employment examinations, medical test processing with reference ranges, inventory tracking, and an internal messaging and notification system.

01

Patient & User Management

Manages patient records, user accounts, and account invitation links for onboarding staff and patients into the system.

02

Appointment & Scheduling

Handles appointment booking, appointment-to-test assignments, and routing of medical tests for streamlined scheduling workflows.

03

Medical Testing & Examinations

Covers OPD examinations, medical tests with categories, items, and reference ranges, plus annual physical and pre-employment examinations with drug test results.

04

Pre-Employment Processing

Dedicated module for pre-employment medical tests, examination records, and pre-employment records to support HR and clinic workflows.

05

Inventory & Medical Checklist

Tracks medical supply inventory and manages medical checklists to ensure clinic readiness and compliance during examinations.

06

Notifications & Messaging

Built-in messaging and notification system keeps patients and staff informed about appointments, results, and system updates.
